Tax deduction methodology: Effective payment reflects estimated monthly tax savings from itemizing versus taking the standard deduction (2026: Single $16,100 · Joint $32,200 · HoH $24,150). Mortgage interest is calculated based on the first full year of ownership. If you purchase a home in 2026, the earliest these deductions can be claimed is on your 2027 federal and state tax returns — you will not see this benefit in your monthly cash flow. Interest paid decreases each year as the loan balance declines, so actual savings will be lower in subsequent years. SALT includes state income taxes and property taxes, combined and capped at $40,000 per the One Big Beautiful Bill Act. Tax savings calculated using federal marginal bracket only. Oregon uses graduated brackets (4.75%–9.90%). This calculator is for educational and informational purposes only and does not constitute tax advice. Please consult with a qualified tax advisor before making any financial decisions based on estimated tax savings.
