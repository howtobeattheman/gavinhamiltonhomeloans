Tax deduction methodology: Effective payment reflects estimated monthly tax savings from itemizing deductions versus taking the standard deduction (2026: Single $16,100 · Joint $32,200 · HoH $24,150). Per IRS Topic 503, SALT includes both state income taxes and property taxes, combined and capped at $40,000 per the One Big Beautiful Bill Act. State income tax is a SALT deductible item — it reduces your federal taxable income, generating a federal tax savings only. Tax savings are calculated using your federal marginal bracket only. Oregon state income tax uses graduated brackets (4.75%–9.90%). California uses graduated brackets (1.00%–9.30%+ for income below $1M) per CA FTB Schedule X/Y/Z, with personal exemption credit applied. Washington has no state income tax — only property taxes are included in SALT for WA residents. Rent provides no federal tax deduction benefit. This is an estimate — consult a tax professional for personalized advice.
